who we are

Wiring with Women (WWW) is an undergraduate organization at Duke University dedicated to supporting women in computing. We aim to build and strengthen a supportive community for women in computer science and engineering, provide education and professional opportunities, and raise awareness  around the issues women in the field face.

why we care

WWW strives to work towards closing this gender gap, encouraging diversity in the technology industry, and empowering underrepresented groups in computer science.
