August 22- Duke SPIRE Fellows

spotlight

The Duke Technology Scholars Program is an initiative at Duke University dedicated to ensuring that more women choose careers in computer science and electrical & computer engineering. Participants receive mentors, gain professional experience, build their network, and receive funding for an internship in either Silicon Valley/San Francisco or Research Triangle Park. Check out the blog posts written by the 2017 DTech Scholars to learn more about their experience!
